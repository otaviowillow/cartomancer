Glossary

  • Monster: Enemy AI
  • Querent: Player controlled character
  • Charm Cards: Charm cards are temporary cards, or cards with effects that don't proc every turn
  • Curse Cards: Curse cards are negative effects for the player that proc every turn. They last until the end of the game or until removed
  • Blessing Cards: Blessing cards are positive effects that proc every turn. They last until the end of the game or until removed
